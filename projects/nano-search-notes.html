Nano Search Notes

Notes on a minimal crawler + BM25 search CLI.

Goal

Asynchronous crawler and cleaner that writes cleaned text to Parquet, then ranks content with BM25.

Flow

Crawl feeds, strip boilerplate (HTML, scripts, styles), store cleaned URLs + content, index, then query with BM25.

Ranking

BM25 with IDF and length normalization; future slots for other ranking (e.g., PageRank).

Ideas

Concurrent crawling/indexing and a small UI to browse results.
