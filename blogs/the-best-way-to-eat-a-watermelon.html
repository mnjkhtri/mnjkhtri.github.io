The Best Way to Eat a Watermelon

May 17, 2026

I will tell you the steps. Not because this is complicated, but because watermelon deserves a little structure.

Get a good one

Look for the yellow patch. That is usually a good sign. If the watermelon looks too perfect, do not trust it too much.

Check the seeds

Cut it open and look at the seeds. White seeds are not the vibe. You want the watermelon to look like it actually finished loading.

Cube it

Cut it into cubes and remove the seeds. This is the only slightly annoying part, but future you will be grateful.

Freeze it

Put the cubes in the freezer for two to three hours. Not forever. You are not making watermelon bricks.

Eat it

Take it out when it is cold, firm, and still biteable. That is the whole trick. Frozen-ish watermelon is elite.
